Thun

Background
"My first memory is that of annoying light, irritating against my eyes and worsening the dreadful headache. I tried shouting for someone 'Turn off the sun!', but alas, all that my hoarse voice could carry was '... Thun...?!'. Not really something most would believe as the origin of a name, but still so. I've learnt to live without the memories of my past, instead making new ones as I go, travelling across the land and searching for more tales to tell of!"
